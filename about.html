About CMAPP

Why CMapp?

Best practices increase productivity for Coffee plantations and can be enforced in all production processes, even over multiple Coffee plantations using different Coffee plantation standards producing different varieties of Coffee

Reduce plantation wide risk by guaranteeing easy to record and highly accurate traceability systems

Rapid management response improves Coffee plantation profit via on-screen and emailed alerts, improved reporting, mobile monitoring, and easy Coffee plantation wide monitoring and observation systems

Improve Coffee plantation cash flow management information flows from the budget monitoring systems, allowing Coffee plantation managers to easily assess plantation progress analyzed by Coffee variety, site, or Coffee plantation area

Reduced material & labour costs This is a direct result of accurate capture and monitoring of employee labor, and inventory management

Pass audits with ease by having plantation wide traceability and operations information, at your fingertips in a useful format; at all times

Minimize material waste and shrinkage on Coffee plantations via easy to use inventory management processes that incorporate traceability processes for Coffee plantation

Improve communication between Coffee plantation teams via accurate task management tools, alert systems, and printing tasks.

Reduced administration costs resulting from easy collection and reporting of costs and traceability information

Enhance cash flow and profit via easy to use, comprehensive planning of resources and Coffee plantation activities

Stop employees from forgetting Pre Harvest Intervals (PHI) and re-entry periods by automatic (configurable) alerts and critical stops in task management systems
